The Best Nana Cakes In Lynnwood

Melt in your mouth banana flap jacks

This recipes was handed down to me by my great grand father.

Which in turn was handed down to him from his great uncle (twice removed).

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • 1 Egg
  • Milk
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Mix- Combine your dry ingredients (flour, sugar, salt, baking powder) in one bowl and your wet ingredients (egg, milk, vegetable oil, mashed bananas) in another bowl. Add the dry ingredients to the bowl with the wet ingredients, then stir until they're incorporated. It's OK if your batter is slightly lumpy.
  2. Cook- Pour the batter in Â¼ cup portions onto a lightly oiled pan or griddle over medium-high heat. Cook for a few minutes, flip with a spatula, and cook for another few minutes (or until each side is golden brown).
  3. Serve- Serve your banana pancakes immediately. They're delicious alone or with your favorite pancake toppings.
